6 REASONS ALL TRAVELLERS NEED THE NEW IPHONE 6

By Veera Papinoja

Yesterday the whole word sat down to watch as Apple launched its new iPhone 6, iPhone 6 Plus and Apple watch device. I may not be the tech guy in the house, but I sure am the traveller and madly excited about the new iPhone 6. Stay tuned for how my Instagram feed will approve and perhaps CreateTrips will finally get its own YouTube channel!

1. Better travel selfies

Burst selfies is the new deal -now you can make sure to get the perfect shot of your pretty face meditating next to the Taj Mahal or sipping down that champagne by the Eiffel Tower. Finally; technology is on our side and your Facebook feed will look more fabulous than ever before!


This is H2 and the upper title is H1

Some might argue on this, but I once did not get to travel to Tokyo as all my credit cards were stolen when I was celebrating New Years in China. Now you can just snap a photo of your credit card and use it to pay for your next Starbucks latte. Apple Pay enables cashless and cardless shopping.
